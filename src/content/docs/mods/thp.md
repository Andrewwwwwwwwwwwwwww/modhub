title: The Hungering Portal
description: Feed the End portal a ritual of offerings and a gathering of players, then face a deadlier, multi-phase dragon.
---

The End portal no longer just opens — it **hungers**. Lay a ritual of rare offerings at the frame with enough players present as witnesses, and only then does the way open to a reworked, multi-phase Ender Dragon. Server-configurable, fully localized (English and Traditional Chinese), and shipped as a datapack-in-jar.
