---
title: Villager Shop
description: Player-run villager shops with stock, payment storage and a trade editor.
---

Let players set up their own villager-run shops. Owners stock goods, set trades and prices in an in-game editor, and collect payments from a storage screen. Fully localized (English and Traditional Chinese).
