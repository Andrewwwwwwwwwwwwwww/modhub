---
title: Fallen
description: A lootable corpse holds your items and XP on death until you reclaim it.
---

When you die, your body stays where you fell — a corpse wearing your skin that holds everything you were carrying plus your XP. Walk back and reclaim it; items return to their original slots. Includes a death-history screen for moderation. See also the [Fallen: Backpacked](/modhub/mods/fallen-backpacked/) add-on.
