A small compatibility add-on for **[Fallen](/modhub/mods/fallen/)** and **MrCrayfish's Backpacked**. Backpacked keeps backpacks in their own slots, outside your normal inventory — so without this, they'd be left on the ground when you die while everything else went into your body. Install this and your worn backpacks (and everything inside them) go into your corpse, and are handed back when you reclaim it.

Only useful if you run both mods.

## Requirements

- **Fallen** and **MrCrayfish's Backpacked**
- **Fabric** with Fabric API, Minecraft **26.1.2**
- Install on both the client and the server.

## How it works

- On death, your equipped backpacks are moved into the corpse instead of dropping. Because Backpacked stores a backpack's contents inside the item itself, the whole thing — bag and everything in it — travels together.
- Reclaim them from the body one at a time, or all at once with the corpse screen's Transfer button. If a backpack can't be re-equipped (no free slot), it's handed to your inventory so nothing is lost.
- If a body holds more backpacks than the screen can show at once, hovering the slot tells you how many are stored.
