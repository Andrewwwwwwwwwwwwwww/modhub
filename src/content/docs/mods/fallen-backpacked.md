---
title: "Fallen: Backpacked"
description: Compatibility add-on so Backpacked backpacks go into your Fallen corpse.
---

A small add-on for [Fallen](/modhub/mods/fallen/) and MrCrayfish's Backpacked: your worn backpacks (and their contents) go into your corpse on death instead of dropping, and are handed back when you reclaim it. Install only if you use both mods.
